## Thesis

Rotate into crypto-adjacent equities now that the halving cycle plus sustained spot-ETF inflows
are re-rating the space. Miners and exchanges/brokers with direct crypto exposure should benefit
disproportionately as institutional flows keep coming in through the ETF wrapper, on top of the
supply-side squeeze from the halving reducing new issuance. I'd fund this by trimming some of my
more defensive equity exposure — the thesis is that this is a multi-quarter re-rating, not a quick
trade, so I'm willing to hold through volatility to let the flows and the halving supply effect
play out.
